Analysis of movie ratings of fandango website

Analysed the ratings of website 'Fandango' which sells movie tickets and compared it with the ratings of websites which dont sell movie tickets such as IMDB,MetaCritic and Rotten Tomatoes.Arrived to following conclusions after analysis:-
-Movie rating of Fandango was higher than ratings of IMDB, MetaCritic and Rotten Tomatoes
-The movies having rating less than 3 in IMDB, MetaCritic and Rotten Tomatoes had more than 3 rating in Fandango. There were no movies in Fandango which had a rating below 3.
-In fandango’s movie reviews ratings were always rounded up for example: 3.6 was rounded up to 4 but even 3.1 was rounded up to 4.

Laser Based Vehicle Guidance

The laser based vehicle guidance system consists of a laser control station and an automated guided vehicle (AGV). The laser control station as the name suggests is used for controlling the laser and the automated guided vehicle (AGV ) follows the laser being controlled by the laser control station. The coordinates to be traversed by the automated guided vehicle sent to the laser control station from the master control station using socket communication wirelessly.
